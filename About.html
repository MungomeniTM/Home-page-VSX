About VSXchangeZA

VSXchangeZA was founded by Tshidzula Mulisa Mungomeni, a visionary who believes that "the future belongs to those who learn more skills and combine them in creative ways."

This platform was developed to address the vocational skills gap that should have been introduced at an early age in our education system. We believe vocational training should be a core part of the South African school curriculum, empowering young people with practical skills they can use to build their future.
